How do you get involved

All Members are encouraged to engage with the Plan process so that we can deliver a shared vision for the future of our Institute.

To be successful, the Institute must capture the full range of Membership views. In the coming months, we will be conducting a comprehensive survey of Members and will be arranging a series of workshops, stakeholder consultations and interviews. Both an issues Paper and a Draft Corporate and Business Plan will be made available to all Members for their comments, analysis and suggestions.
